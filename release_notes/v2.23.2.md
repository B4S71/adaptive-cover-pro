# v2.23.2

Adds a dedicated sun-tracking minimum position for roller shutters and similar covers with a mechanical dead zone at the bottom of travel.

---

## ✨ Features

### Separate sun-tracking minimum position ([#467], [#468])

Covers with a mechanical dead zone — roller shutters whose slats gap open before the bottom bar lifts, for example — need a higher floor during sun tracking than at end-of-day close. The new `CONF_MIN_POSITION_SUN_TRACKING` option (`min_position_sun_tracking`) satisfies this: when the sun is in the field of view, `PositionConverter.apply_limits()` uses `sun_tracking_min_pos` as the effective floor instead of `min_pos`; when the sun leaves or the field is invalid, the original `CONF_MIN_POSITION` floor resumes, so sunset-close still reaches fully closed. The field is stored as `CoverConfig.min_pos_sun_tracking` (`int | None`, default `None`) and read via `CoverConfig.from_options()` without an `or` fallback so that a user-set `0` is preserved rather than collapsed to `None`. The option surfaces in the position-limits step of the config flow as a 0–99% slider, is exported by `async_handle_export()`, and appears in the `DiagnosticsBuilder` config snapshot under `"min_position_sun_tracking"`. When sun-tracking min is set below the always-on min position, `_build_config_summary()` emits a warning — the always-on floor dominates in that state, making the sun-tracking floor a no-op.
